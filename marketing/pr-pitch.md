# MukGo — PR Pitch Kit

*The hook: "the app that rates America's restrooms." Journalists buy stories, not apps.*
*Rules: pitch one journalist at a time (never mass-BCC), reference their beat, keep it
under 150 words, offer exclusives to tier-1 targets first.*

---

| Angle | One-liner | Best for |

## 5. Press release boilerplate (for the launch moment)

**Headline options:**
- `MukGo launches the first driver-built cleanliness ratings for America's road stops`
- `New app MukGo shows drivers only the stops ahead — and whether the restroom is clean`

**Subhead:** `Free app rates restroom and food-area cleanliness at gas stations and
restaurants, recommends only stops ahead on your route, and launches in five languages.`

**Boilerplate paragraph (reuse everywhere):**
```
MukGo is a decision engine for drivers. Instead of hundreds of pins in every
direction, MukGo shows the five best food and gas stops ahead on a driver's route,
with restroom and food-area cleanliness rated by real drivers — data available on no
other map application. Founded after hundreds of thousands of miles of American
road travel, MukGo is live on Google Play in five languages. Gas search is free and
unlimited. Learn more at [site].
```

**Founder quote (approved):**
> "Drivers don't need more information — they need better decisions. You can learn more
> about a business from its restroom than from its advertising, and until now no app
> measured that. MukGo does."

**Stats block (with sources):** 15% restroom acceptability (US consumer survey) · 80%
won't return after a dirty restroom / 95% avoid entirely (Cintas) · 62% spend more where
restrooms are clean (Harris Poll) · 240M US licensed drivers · $213B US road-trip market.

## 8. Sequencing & discipline

| Phase | Action |
|---|---|
| Pre-launch (now) | Build a 30-name media list (10 tech, 10 travel/lifestyle, 5 AAPI/Korean, 5 podcasts). Set HARO/Qwoted alerts. |
| Launch week −7 | Offer the exclusive to ONE tier-1 target. 48h to respond, then move down the list. |
| Launch day | Press release + PH launch same morning. Pitch tier-2 broadly (individually written). |
| Launch +1–4 wks | Podcast circuit + seasonal/segment pitches. Recycle any coverage into social proof everywhere. |

**Never:** attach the press release to a first pitch (link it) · pitch two competing
outlets an "exclusive" simultaneously · follow up more than twice · send the same email
to two journalists at the same outlet.

**Measure:** replies (not opens) · pieces published · domain authority of coverage ·
install spikes on coverage days (UTM the press link).
