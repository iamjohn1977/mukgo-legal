### Copy rules applied
- **Hero leads with the outcome** ("Every stop, worth it"), not the mechanism — mechanism
  comes one line down. Higher conversion than a feature headline.
- **Problem before product** (Sections 3→4): the "same drive" contrast mirrors the pitch deck,
  so web and investor story reinforce each other.
- **The paywall is reframed as safety** ("gas always unlimited") everywhere it appears — a
  trust signal, not a limitation.
- **Numbers only where sequential** (Section 7). No decorative 01/02/03 elsewhere.
- **One promise, repeated:** "Stop with confidence" appears in hero-adjacent copy, Section 4,
  and the footer — the through-line that ties the whole site to app store + pitch.
