How to delete your MukGo account data — with or without deleting your account

This page explains how users of the MukGo mobile application can request deletion of the personal data MukGo has collected. You can delete your data at any time, and you do not need to delete your account to remove specific data.

Option 1 — Delete in the app

If you created a MukGo account, open the app and go to Account → Delete account. This permanently removes your account and all data tied to it (see the table below).

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

To delete some or all of your data — including if you joined the waitlist but never created an account — send us a request:

  1. Email privacy@alpspira.com with the subject "MukGo Data Deletion Request."
  2. Include the email address you used with MukGo (or the waitlist), so we can locate your data.
  3. Tell us whether you want all of your data deleted, or only specific items (for example, only your gas-station ratings).

We verify the request comes from the email owner and complete deletion within 30 days. We'll confirm by email when it's done.
