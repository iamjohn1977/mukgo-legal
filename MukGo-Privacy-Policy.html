Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 21, 2026  ·  Applies to the MukGo mobile application and website
The short version: MukGo runs on very little personal data. Your location is used live to find places near you and is not kept as a trip history. We do not sell your data, we do not show targeted ads, and there is no third-party analytics, tracking or advertising SDK in the app. You can have your account and data deleted by email at any time.

1.Who we are

MukGo ("we," "us," or "our") is a driving companion app operated by ALPSPIRA, 18327 Gresham St, Northridge, CA 91324, USA. This Privacy Policy explains what the MukGo app collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. Questions and requests: privacy@alpspira.com.

3.Location — how it is actually used

Location answers one question: what is near you right now. It is sent to our mapping provider to fetch nearby places, and it is used on your device to estimate walking times and to detect whether the car is moving, so that screens needing a lot of typing can lock themselves while you drive.

We do not build or store a history of where you have driven. Coordinates are not stored on our servers except where they are attached to a review you deliberately post, or as the coarse location of an anonymous stop rating (section 5). You can revoke location permission at any time in your phone settings; the app still works, with fewer features.

4.AI stop planning

When you tap the AI planner, we send Anthropic — our AI provider — only these four things: how many minutes you have, the names and categories of the nearby places already shown on your screen, their computed round-trip walking times, and your display language.

We do not send your name, email address, account ID, or coordinates. The AI orders and explains options; it neither receives nor produces personal data about you. Anthropic processes this as our service provider and, under its API terms, does not use it to train models.

5.Anonymous stop feedback and safety consent

When you answer whether a stop was worth it, we store the answer with the place, a coarse location and the random device identifier — not your account. When you accept the safety warning on first run, we store that acceptance with its date and time, the app version of the warning, and the same device identifier.

Both records are write-only from the app: they are used in aggregate (or, for consent, as a record that the warning was shown) and cannot be read back or tied to you through the app.

7.What we do not do

  • No selling or renting of personal data
  • No targeted advertising
  • No third-party analytics, tracking or ad SDKs in the app
  • No audio or video recording
  • No tracking you across other apps or websites
  • No stored history of your trips

8.Keeping and deleting your data

Account data is kept while your account exists. Reviews stay visible while they are useful to other drivers; when your account is deleted they are removed or permanently detached from you. Anonymous feedback and consent records are retained and cannot be reversed to identify you.

To delete your account and its data, or to request a copy of it, email privacy@alpspira.com from the address on your account. We verify the request and act within 30 days. Full instructions are on our Data Deletion page.

Depending on where you live you may have additional rights — access, correction, portability, objection, and the right to complain to your data protection authority. We honour these requests regardless of where you live. California residents may exercise rights under the CCPA/CPRA at the same address; we do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined.

9.Children

MukGo is not designed for children and is not directed to anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has given us data, email privacy@alpspira.com and we will delete it.

10.International transfers and changes

Our providers may process data in countries other than yours, including the United States, using the safeguards those providers offer. If we change this policy in a material way we will announce it in the app and update the effective date above.
