Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026  ·  Applies to the MukGo mobile application
The short version: MukGo uses your location only to find restaurants and road-trip stops near you or along your route. We don't sell your data, we don't show ads, and we don't share your information with anyone except the services that make the app work. You can delete your profile at any time from inside the app.

1.Who we are

MukGo ("we," "us," or "our") is a road-trip food and stop finder app operated by ALPSPIRA, 18327 Gresham St, Northridge, CA 91324, USA. This Privacy Policy explains what information the MukGo app collects, how we use it, and the choices you have.

2.Information we collect

We do not collect your contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or background location. We do not use your data for advertising.

3.How your information is used

  • To search for and display restaurants, cafes, gas stations, rest areas, and other points of interest relevant to your trip
  • To save your taste profile so recommendations improve over time
  • To launch navigation in the map app you choose
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app performance

5.Data storage and retention

Your profile is stored locally on your device and, when available, synced to our secure Supabase database so it can be restored if you reinstall the app. We keep your profile only for as long as you use MukGo. If our servers are unreachable, the app continues to work using data stored on your device only.

6.Your choices and rights

  • Delete your data: Use "Clear profile & start fresh" inside the app to erase your profile. You may also email us to request deletion of any synced data.
  • Location permission: You can revoke location access at any time in your device settings. The app will still work if you type a destination manually.
  • Access and correction: You may contact us to request a copy of the data we hold about you, or to correct it.

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale of personal information. MukGo does not sell personal information.

7.Children's privacy

MukGo is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8.Security

We use industry-standard safeguards, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all network traffic between the app and our services. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information appropriately.

9.Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the effective date above and notify you inside the app. Continued use of MukGo after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.
